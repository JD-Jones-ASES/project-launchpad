Study Slides

Key Concepts (1/2)

Virtù

Refers to human skill, energy, decisiveness, talent, and strength of character. It encompasses the qualities a prince needs to act effectively and strategically in the political sphere.

Fortuna

Represents fortune, luck, chance, or uncontrollable circumstances. These are the external forces and unpredictable events that can impact political outcomes, often beyond human control.

The Problem of Fortune

The central philosophical dilemma Machiavelli addresses: if powerful leaders can be overthrown by events outside their control, is human effort in politics ultimately futile?

Key Concepts (2/2)

Fortune Favors the Bold

Machiavelli argues that decisive, bold action is often more successful than cautious approaches, partly because it matches the demands of most political crises and demonstrates adaptability.
