New Principalities and the Exercise of Virtù

Study Slides

The Interaction of Virtù and Fortuna

Fortuna (Opportunity)

The specific historical circumstances, crises, or moments of instability that make a population willing to accept a new leader.

Virtù (Ability/Talent)

The exceptional personal qualities required to recognize that opportunity and give it lasting political form.

The Crisis as Raw Material

Moses

found the Israelites enslaved.

Cyrus

found the Persians discontented.

Romulus

found Rome in chaos.

Theseus

found the Athenians scattered.

The Difficulty of the "New Order"

Active Enemies

Those who benefited from the old system will fight the reformer with ferocity.

Timid Supporters

Those who might benefit from the new system offer only lukewarm support because they are uncertain of the outcome and fear the established powers.

The Unarmed Prophet: Girolamo Savonarola

Achievement

Expelled the Medici and instituted moral and religious reforms.

Failure

When his support collapsed following excommunication by Pope Alexander VI, he had no army to protect his position.

Outcome

He was arrested, tortured, and executed in the same square where he had held his "Bonfires of the Vanities."
