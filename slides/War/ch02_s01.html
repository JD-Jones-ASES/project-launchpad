Key Terms

Ethos

— A speaker's credibility, derived from character and experience. Butler's ethos comes from his combat record, not his education.

Banana Wars

— U.S. military interventions in Latin America and the Caribbean (roughly 1898–1934), often conducted on behalf of American business interests.

Gendarmerie d'Haïti

— Military police force established during the American occupation of Haiti, which Butler commanded.

Camp Pontanezen

— The largest American debarkation camp in Europe during WWI, commanded by Butler in France.
