Chapter 5, Section 3

Talking Past Each Other

Study Slides

What Remarque Refuses to Do

• Holmes says war builds character. Remarque shows what war builds: shell craters and corpses.

• Wilson says war defends democracy. Remarque shows what war defends: a few hundred yards of mud.

• Butler says war enriches the few. Remarque does not care who profits — he cares about who dies.
