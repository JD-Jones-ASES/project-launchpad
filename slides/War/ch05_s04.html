What the Excerpt Reveals

Chance

The Limits of Argument

Holmes

The passage describes not heroic endurance but desperate survival. The soldiers are not choosing to be brave — they are trying not to die.

Wilson

The German soldiers suffer identically to Allied soldiers and were told the same things about duty and necessity by their own leaders.

Butler

For the men in the trench, the question of who profits is as distant as the question of who is morally right. Their world has shrunk to the shell crater and the next few seconds of life.
