Study Slides

Proposal 2: A Limited Plebiscite

Constitutional

The U.S. Constitution grants Congress the power to declare war. A plebiscite would require a constitutional amendment — a high bar.

Speed

Wars sometimes begin with surprise attacks. Could a nation hold a national vote while enemy forces were advancing?

Assumption

History suggests young men do not always vote against war. In 1917, many were eager to fight; in 1941, after Pearl Harbor, the desire to go to war was nearly universal across all age groups.
