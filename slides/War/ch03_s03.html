Key Concepts (1/2)

When and to whom did Wilson deliver this address, and what happened four days later?

Wilson delivered it to a joint session of Congress on the evening of April 2, 1917. Four days later, Congress voted to declare war on Germany.

What does the text say Wilson's address demonstrates in terms of its architecture?

It builds from grievance to principle to resolution, acknowledging the gravity of what he is asking before he asks it.
