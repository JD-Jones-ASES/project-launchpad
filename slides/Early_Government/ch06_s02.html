Important Terms

state constitution

A written plan that describes the structure, powers, and limits of a state government.

amendment

A formal change or addition to a constitution.

ratify

To officially approve a law or constitution so it takes effect.

bill of rights

The section of a constitution that lists the rights that government must protect for its citizens.

Texas's Many Constitutions (1/2)

1827

Under Mexican rule (written in Spanish).

1836

For the Republic of Texas (the first Texas constitution in English).

1845

When Texas joined the United States.

1861

Upon joining the Confederacy at the start of the Civil War.

1866

Adopted immediately after the Civil War.

Texas's Many Constitutions (2/2)

1869

During the Reconstruction era, imposed by federal authorities.

1876

The current Texas Constitution, written after Reconstruction ended.
