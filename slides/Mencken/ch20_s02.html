Democracy vs. The Superior Individual

Suppression of Nature

Democracy is described as a history of efforts to force minorities to be "untrue to their nature."

The *Lex de Majestate*

This Roman law is cited as a democratic invention intended to protect the state against aristocrats and free spirits who hold themselves answerable only to their own notions.

Leveling

The goal of democracy is to "iron out" free spirits, pump them dry of self-respect, and make them "docile John Does."
