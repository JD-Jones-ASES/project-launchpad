Key Concepts (1/2)

What is the primary focus of studying H.L. Mencken in this book?

The primary focus is to understand how Mencken builds his arguments through language, rather than judging what he thinks.

What practical skill will students develop by applying the analytical framework to Mencken's writing?

Students will become better readers of any persuasive prose, including editorials, speeches, essays, and political campaigns.
