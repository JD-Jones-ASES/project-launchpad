Glossary of Key Terms (1/2)

Armamentarium

A collection of equipment or methods; used by Mencken to describe the deceptive tools available to a politician.

Clapper-clawing

A term meaning to scratch or claw noisily; used to describe the vulgar, animalistic applause of a crowd.

Demaslave

Mencken’s coinage for a politician who pretends to follow the people while appearing to lead them; a hybrid of "demagogue" and "slave."

Iron Law of Oligarchy

The theory that all organizations eventually become controlled by a small elite group.

Periodic Sentence

A sentence that delays its main point or completion until the very end to build suspense and emphasis.

Glossary of Key Terms (2/2)

The Boss System

A historical American political structure where "city bosses" controlled patronage networks, trading jobs and favors for votes.
