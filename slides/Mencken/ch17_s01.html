The Residency Requirement and Structural Mediocrity

Constitutional Basis

Article I, Sections 2 and 3 require members of the House and Senate to be inhabitants of the state they represent. By custom, House members must also reside within their specific districts.

The Two Registers of Language

Register. Examples

Purpose

Legalistic/Constitutional. "Article I, Sections 2 and 3," "electoral unit," "legislative bodies"

Mimics the dignity of civics textbooks to create a mock-serious atmosphere.

Abusive/Invective. "Puerile," "imbeciles," "jackasses," "noodles," "vermin"

Strips away the "costume" of government to reveal the perceived reality of the individuals involved.
