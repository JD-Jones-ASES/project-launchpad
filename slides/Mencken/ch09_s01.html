Glossary of Important Terms

Term. Definition

Contextual Usage

Ameliorating. Making something better; improving conditions.

Used to describe reforms that the "mob" paradoxically resists.

Calaboose. A jail or prison (from the Spanish *calabozo*).

A colloquial term used to deflate the gravity of the mob's tyranny.

Cosmogonies. Theories regarding the origin of the universe.

Contrasted as "inordinately complex" against the simplicity of Genesis.

Enumeration. The act of mentioning a number of things one by one; a list.

Used as a "syntax of indictment" to overwhelm with evidence of ignorance.

Farrago. A confused mixture or hodgepodge.

Used to reduce Fundamentalism to a collection of intellectual debris.

Hookworm Belt. Mencken’s term for the rural South.

Represents an area of endemic disease and impoverished intellectual life.

Periodic Sentence. A sentence that delays its main clause until the end.

Creates suspense and makes the final "verdict" feel inevitable.
