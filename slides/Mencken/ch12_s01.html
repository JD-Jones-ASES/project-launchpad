H.L. Mencken on "The Popular Will"

Study Slides

Key Concepts and Rhetorical Analysis

The Electorate

Described as "sheep" (docile), "donkeys" (stubborn/stupid), and "goats" (gullible victims).

The Conflict

Defined as a "combat between jackals and jackasses." The politicians are the predators (jackals) and the voters are the beasts of burden (jackasses).
