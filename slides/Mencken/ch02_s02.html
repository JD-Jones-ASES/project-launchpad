Key Concepts and Themes

The Intellectual View

The people possess a "seraphic intuition" and a "reservoir of righteousness" capable of solving any problem.

The Reality of the Mob

Historical Precedent

During the French Revolution, the Paris proletariat killed their King only to eventually kill the leaders who misled them, ultimately welcoming a new King and aristocracy back into power by 1796.
