Important Terms (1/2)

Bootlegger

A person who makes, distributes, or sells alcoholic beverages illegally. This term is used in rumors about Gatsby, suggesting his wealth comes from illicit activities during Prohibition.

Knickerbockers

Loose-fitting breeches gathered at the knee. Clarence Endive is noted for wearing them, connecting him to a particular fashion trend or social class.

Rajah

An Indian king or prince; a term Gatsby uses to describe his opulent lifestyle in Europe, evoking images of exotic luxury.

Montenegro

Argonne Forest

A significant battleground in northeastern France during World War I. Gatsby claims to have performed extraordinary feats of bravery there, cementing his image as a war hero.
