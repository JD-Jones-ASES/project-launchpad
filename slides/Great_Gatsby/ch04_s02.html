Montenegro Medal (Order of Danilo)

A genuine war decoration awarded to Gatsby by the government of Montenegro, which he uses as physical proof to support his claims of war heroism.

Oxford Photograph

A photograph showing Gatsby among young men at Oxford University, used as further physical evidence to validate his claims of an elite European education.

Rosy Rosenthal

A man murdered at the old Metropole, whose death Meyer Wolfshiem recounts with a strange mix of sentimentality and callousness, highlighting the violence inherent in his world.

The infamous baseball championship series that Meyer Wolfshiem allegedly "fixed" or manipulated, establishing his reputation as a powerful figure in organized crime.
