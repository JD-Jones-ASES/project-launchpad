Creditworthiness

A measure of your ability and willingness to repay borrowed money. It's what lenders and others assess when they look at your credit score and report.

FICO Score

The most widely used credit score model, created by the Fair Isaac Corporation. Most banks, car dealerships, and mortgage companies rely on some version of the FICO score to evaluate applicants.

VantageScore

Another common credit score model, developed collaboratively by the three major credit bureaus.
