Important Terms

Beneficiary

The person or entity designated to receive assets from a life insurance policy, retirement account, or other financial accounts upon the owner's death.

529 Plan

A state-sponsored, tax-advantaged investment vehicle designed to help families save for future education expenses. Contributions are made with after-tax dollars, but the account grows tax-free, and...

Guardian

A person legally appointed to care for another person (such as a minor child) or their property.

Qualified Education Expenses

Specific expenses that count towards tax-free withdrawals from a 529 plan, including tuition, fees, books, supplies, equipment, and room and board at an eligible educational institution.

Future Value of an Annuity

A financial calculation that determines the total value of a series of equal payments (annuity) over a period, considering compound interest.
