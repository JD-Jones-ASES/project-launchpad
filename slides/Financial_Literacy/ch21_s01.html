Why Risk Pooling Works

For the Individual

You exchange a small, predictable, and manageable cost (your premium) for protection against a potentially devastating, unpredictable financial loss.

For the Insurer

By collecting premiums from thousands of customers, and knowing statistically that only a fraction will file claims, the insurer can accurately assess risk and maintain financial viability.

For Society

Insurance helps to mitigate widespread financial instability and hardship that would otherwise occur from unexpected events, contributing to overall economic resilience.
