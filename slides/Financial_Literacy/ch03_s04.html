Monthly Savings Formula

The core calculation for reverse planning: `Monthly Savings = Goal Amount / Number of Months`. This simple division reveals the consistent amount you must save each month.

For simplicity and to establish a baseline, this section's formula assumes no interest is earned on your savings. Future learning will incorporate the benefits of interest.

Important Terms

Reverse Planning

The process of starting with a target financial goal and a deadline, then working backward to calculate the specific monthly savings amount needed to reach that goal.

Monthly Savings Required

The calculated amount of money that must be saved each month to achieve a specific financial goal by its designated deadline, assuming no interest is earned.
