Important Terms (1/2)

Active Income

Income earned in exchange for time and effort, requiring the earner's direct and ongoing participation. Examples include wages, salary, tips, commissions, and most self-employment income.

Passive Income

Earnings derived from assets or activities that do not require the earner's continuous active participation. Examples include rental income, dividends, interest, royalties, and income from limited ...

Rental Income

Money received from tenants for the use of a property you own.

Dividends

Quarterly or periodic payments made by a company to its shareholders out of its profits.

Interest

Money earned on savings, investments (like Certificates of Deposit or bonds), or from lending money.

Important Terms (2/2)

Royalties

Ongoing payments made for the use of intellectual property, such as a book, song, or patent, that you created.

Financial Independence

The state of having enough passive income to cover one's living expenses, thereby eliminating the need to work for money.
