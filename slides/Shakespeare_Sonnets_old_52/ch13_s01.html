A term with dual meaning in this context: 1) A final tallying of debts, consequences, or accounts. 2) A moral confrontation with the results of one's actions.

Forsworn/Perjured

The Nature of "Reckoning"

Accounting Sense

A final tallying of all the emotional, moral, and spiritual "debts" accumulated throughout the tumultuous relationship.

Moral Sense

A direct confrontation with the painful consequences and ethical failures stemming from desire and entanglement.
