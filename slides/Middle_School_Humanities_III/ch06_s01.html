Major Engineering Feat

Opened in 1869, connecting the Mediterranean and Red Seas through the Isthmus of Suez in Egypt.

Dramatic Route Reduction

Strategic Importance

Of immense strategic and commercial value to the British Empire, which controlled India and parts of East Africa and Asia.
