What was Elizabeth I's philosophy regarding private religious beliefs?

She famously refused to 'open windows into men's souls,' prioritizing outward conformity to the Church of England over private belief to maintain peace.

How did the Elizabethan Settlement contribute to stability in England compared to other European nations?

For most of Elizabeth's reign, England avoided the religious civil wars that devastated France and the Holy Roman Empire due to the compromise.

Who was Mary Queen of Scots and why was she a threat to Elizabeth I?

Mary was Elizabeth's Catholic cousin with a plausible claim to the English throne, serving as a focal point for various Catholic plots against Elizabeth.

What event prompted Philip II of Spain to launch the Spanish Armada?

The execution of Mary Queen of Scots in February 1587 enraged Philip II, who had already planned to invade England and return it to Rome.
