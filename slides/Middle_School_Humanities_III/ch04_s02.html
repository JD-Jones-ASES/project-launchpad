According to Locke, how does government legitimately acquire the authority to make and enforce laws?

Through the consent of the governed. People agree to live under common rules for mutual protection, forming a social contract.

What real-world event primarily inspired Voltaire to write Candide?

The great Lisbon earthquake of 1755, which killed 30,000 people and challenged philosophical optimism.
