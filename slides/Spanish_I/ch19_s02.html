Gender-Neutral Profession Nouns

Some Spanish nouns for professions use the same form for both masculine and feminine. The only thing that changes to indicate gender is the definite article (`el` for masculine, `la` for feminine).

Expressing Career Aspirations

Crucial Rule

Unlike in English (e.g., "I want to be *a* doctor"), you **do not use an indefinite article** (`un` or `una`) before the profession noun.
