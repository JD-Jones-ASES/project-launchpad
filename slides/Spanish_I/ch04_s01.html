Key Concepts

Colors as Adjectives

In Spanish, color words are adjectives. This means they modify nouns and must agree with them in gender and number.

Cultural Spotlight: "De colores" — A Song for Spring

Theme

The song celebrates the vibrant colors of nature in springtime—fields, birds, rainbows—using them to symbolize joy, hope, and the richness of life.

Origin

Its authorship is unknown; it is a traditional song passed down through generations.

Context

Often heard at celebrations (*fiestas*), in schools, and at community gatherings.

Vocabulary Review

The song is an excellent way to review color vocabulary. It explicitly mentions the colors of the fields, birds, and the rainbow.
