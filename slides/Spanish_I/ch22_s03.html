Celebrating Your Accomplishments: ¡Lo que puedes hacer! (1/3)

Basic Social Interaction

Greet people, say goodbye, and inquire about their well-being.

Description & Identification

Describe yourself and others using *ser* and *estar* with adjectives and locations. Discuss family members, colors, sizes, and appearances with correct adjective agreement.

Daily Life & Routines

Talk about food, daily routines, tell time, and discuss dates.

Expressing Possession & Feelings

Use *tener* expressions, identify body parts, and use possessive adjectives.

Future Plans & Preferences

Discuss going places using *ir + a + infinitive* and express wants, abilities, and preferences with stem-changing verbs like *querer*, *poder*, and *preferir*.

Continuing Your Spanish Journey: ¿Y ahora qué? (1/2)

Find a Conversation Partner (*Encuentra un compañero/a de conversación*)

Regular, even brief, practice with a native speaker is invaluable for fluency and confidence.

Watch Spanish Shows and Movies (*Mira programas y películas en español*)

Start with Spanish subtitles. Animated shows, documentaries, and *telenovelas* are great options to train your ear.

Read Easy Books (*Lee libros fáciles*)

Children's books, comics (*cómics*), or simple story apps can expand your vocabulary and comprehension.

Listen to Music You Love (*Escucha música que te gusta*)

Discover Latin artists in genres you already enjoy. Look up lyrics and sing along to improve pronunciation and natural rhythm.

Visit a Spanish-Speaking Community (*Visita una comunidad hispanohablante*)

Seek out local Spanish restaurants, markets, churches, or cultural events to use your Spanish in real-world contexts. People generally appreciate the effort.
