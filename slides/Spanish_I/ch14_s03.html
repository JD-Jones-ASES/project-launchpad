The dialogue subtly highlights the difference between `saber` (to know a fact/how to do something) and `conocer` (to know a person/place/be familiar with).

The City Walk Memory Technique

An ancient and effective method for vocabulary acquisition, this technique leverages spatial memory by associating new words with landmarks along a mentally imagined route.

Important Terms (1/6)

recordar (o→ue)

To remember. (A stem-changing verb).

contar (o→ue)

To count; to tell (stories). (A stem-changing verb, follows `recordar`'s pattern).

parecer (-zco yo form)

To seem, to appear. (Irregular `yo` form: `parezco`, similar to `conocer` and `crecer`).

Siga derecho

Go straight (formal command from `seguir`).

Doble a la derecha / izquierda

Turn right / left (formal command from `doblar`).
