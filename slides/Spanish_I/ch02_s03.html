Key Concepts (1/2)

What is the Spanish word for 'paper' (and its gender)?

How do you say 'homework' in Spanish (and its gender)?

la tarea (f.)

What is the Spanish word for 'uniform' (and its gender)?

el uniforme (m.)

What are the Spanish words for 'classmate' (male and female)?

el compañero (m.), la compañera (f.)

What is the Spanish word for 'classroom' (and its gender)?

el salón (de clase) (m.)

How do you say 'window' in Spanish (and its gender)?

la ventana (f.)

Key Concepts (2/2)

What is the Spanish word for 'door' (and its gender)?

la puerta (f.)

If you are talking about a mixed group of male and female classmates in Spanish, which plural form should you use?

You should use the masculine plural: los compañeros.

What is the main theme of the traditional Latin American children's rhyme 'Pin Pon'?

It describes a boy named Pin Pon and his daily routine, including washing his face, combing his hair, and going to bed.

Are school uniforms common in Mexican public and private schools?

Yes, uniforms (uniformes) are common in most Mexican public and private schools.

What do Mexican school uniforms typically consist of?

They usually consist of a white shirt or blouse and dark pants or a skirt, often with the school's name or crest.

What are two reasons uniforms are common in Mexican schools?

Uniforms are a source of school pride and make getting dressed in the morning faster.
