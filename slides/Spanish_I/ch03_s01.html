Essential Family Vocabulary

Learning specific Spanish terms for family members is crucial for communication and cultural understanding, as family is a central concept in Spanish-speaking cultures.

Gender Agreement in Plural Forms

In Spanish, when referring to a mixed group of males and females, the masculine plural form of the noun is used (e.g., `los primos` for male and female cousins, `los padres` for parents).
