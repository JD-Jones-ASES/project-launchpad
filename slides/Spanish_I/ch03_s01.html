Culture Focus: La familia en Colombia

Close-Knit Bonds

Colombian families are typically very close, loving, and supportive.

Extended Family Living

It is common for grandparents, aunts, uncles, and cousins to live near each other, and often grandparents (`los abuelos`) even reside in the same house as their children and grandchildren.

Role of Grandparents

Grandparents frequently play an active and vital role in helping to raise the younger generation.

Key Concepts (1/2)

What is the meaning of "Los miembros de la familia"?

Family Members.

In Spanish, when referring to a mixed group of males and females, which gender form is used for the plural?

The masculine plural form.

What does "los primos" mean if the group includes both male and female cousins?

It means 'the cousins' (referring to a mixed group of boys and girls).

What is the more common meaning of "los padres" in Spanish, besides "the fathers"?

It commonly means 'the parents'.

What factor helps clarify the meaning of masculine plural forms like "los primos" or "los padres" when they can refer to mixed groups?

Context.

Historically, what has always been central to life in the Spanish-speaking world?

La familia (the family).

Key Concepts (2/2)

What is "El método de la palabra clave" (The Keyword Method) in language learning?

It's a language learning strategy that connects the sound of a Spanish word to its meaning using a vivid mental image.

What is the first step of the Keyword Method?

Find an English word (or part of a word) that sounds like the Spanish word.

What is the second step of the Keyword Method?

Create a vivid mental image that connects the sound to the meaning.

What is the third step of the Keyword Method?

Every time you see the Spanish word, the image pops up and gives you the meaning.

According to the Keyword Method, why might a 'sillier' mental image be more effective?

The sillier your image, the better it sticks (it's more memorable).

How important is family generally described as being in Colombian culture?

Family is one of the most important parts of life; Colombian families tend to be close and loving.
