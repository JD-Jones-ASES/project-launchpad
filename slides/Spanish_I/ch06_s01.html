The verb phrase used to tell time depends on the hour. Use "Es la" only for 1:00, and "Son las" for all other hours (2:00 and up). Fractions of the hour like "y cuarto" (quarter past) and "y media"...

Time of Day

Specify morning, afternoon, or night using "de la mañana," "de la tarde," and "de la noche" to clarify the time.

Spanish Calendar Conventions

In Spanish-speaking countries, the calendar week typically starts on Monday. Both days of the week and months of the year are not capitalized in Spanish (e.g., *lunes*, *enero*).

Expressing Dates

Dates are formatted as day + "de" + month, with the optional addition of "de" + year (e.g., *el quince de octubre*).
