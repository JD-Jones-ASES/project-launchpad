Important Terms (1/2)

tener

(verb) to have. The central verb of this section.

¡Ojo!

(interjection) Literally "Eye!" Used idiomatically to mean "Watch out!", "Pay attention!", or "Be careful!"

-go ending

A characteristic irregular ending for the "yo" form of several important Spanish verbs, including "tener."

Stem-changing verb (e > ie)

A type of irregular verb where the vowel in the verb stem changes in certain conjugations (in this case, 'e' changes to 'ie'). Also known as a "boot verb" pattern.

lobito

(noun, masculine) little wolf. (From the song "Cinco lobitos")

tetita
