Similar Triangles

Study Slides

Key Concepts

Similar Figures

Geometric figures that have the exact same shape but can differ in size. This means their corresponding angles are congruent, and their corresponding sides are proportional.

Corresponding Parts

In similar figures, the angles and sides that are in the same relative position are called corresponding parts. Matching angles are corresponding angles, and matching sides are corresponding sides.

Proportional Sides

When two figures are similar, the ratio of the lengths of any pair of corresponding sides is constant. This constant ratio is known as the scale factor.

Important Terms

Similar Figures

Figures that have the same shape but not necessarily the same size. Denoted by the symbol $\sim$.

Corresponding Angles

Angles in the same relative position in two different figures (especially similar figures).

Corresponding Sides

Sides in the same relative position in two different figures (especially similar figures), which are opposite corresponding angles.

Proportional

Two quantities are proportional if they have a constant ratio. In similar figures, corresponding sides are proportional.

Identifying Corresponding Parts

Corresponding angles

$R \cong X$, $S \cong Y$, $T \cong Z$.

Corresponding sides

$RS \sim XY$, $ST \sim YZ$, $RT \sim XZ$.

Key Concepts (1/2)

In geometry, what are figures called that have the same shape but not necessarily the same size?

Similar figures.

What are the two main conditions that define similar figures?

Their corresponding angles are congruent, and their corresponding sides are proportional.

In similar figures, what are "corresponding angles" and "corresponding sides"?

Corresponding angles are the matching angles, and corresponding sides are the matching sides.

If two triangles are named ABC ~ DEF, how do you determine which angles correspond?

The order of the letters indicates correspondence: Angle A corresponds to Angle D, Angle B corresponds to Angle E, Angle C corresponds to Angle F.

If two triangles are named ABC ~ DEF, how do you determine which sides correspond?

The sides opposite corresponding angles are corresponding sides: Side BC corresponds to Side EF, Side AC corresponds to Side DF, Side AB corresponds to Side DE.

Given that RST ~ XYZ, list all pairs of corresponding angles.

Angle R corresponds to Angle X, Angle S corresponds to Angle Y, Angle T corresponds to Angle Z.

Key Concepts (2/2)

Given that RST ~ XYZ, list all pairs of corresponding sides.

Side RS corresponds to Side XY, Side ST corresponds to Side YZ, Side RT corresponds to Side XZ.

State the Angle-Angle (AA) Similarity Postulate.

If two angles of one triangle are congruent to two angles of another triangle, then the triangles are similar.

According to the AA Similarity Postulate, what is the minimum number of angle pairs you need to show are congruent to prove two triangles are similar?

Two pairs of corresponding angles.

Why is showing only two pairs of corresponding angles are congruent sufficient to prove triangle similarity?

Because the sum of angles in any triangle is 180 degrees, if two pairs of angles match, the third pair must automatically also match.
