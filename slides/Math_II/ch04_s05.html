Important Terms

Least Common Denominator (LCD)

For a set of fractions, this is the smallest positive integer that is a multiple of all the denominators. It is the key to efficiently clearing fractions from an equation.

Power of 10

Numbers like 10, 100, 1000, and so on. These are used in decimal clearing to shift decimal points to the right, converting decimal numbers into integers.

Coefficient

A numerical factor that multiplies a variable (e.g., in $5x$, 5 is the coefficient). Clearing fractions or decimals aims to make these coefficients integers.

Term

Equivalent Equation

An equation that has the exact same solution set as another equation, even if they look different. Clearing fractions or decimals creates equivalent equations.
