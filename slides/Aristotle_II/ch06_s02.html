What is the 'highest bar' Longinus sets for the orator who understands emotional psychology?

Not merely the persuasion of the audience, but its transport—a state of being overwhelmed and elevated.

How did Philip Sidney extend Aristotle's account of pathos?

Sidney extended it outward into a theory of literature's moral function, arguing that poetry's value lies in its ability to 'move the will' to virtuous action.

List and define the three Ciceronian duties of an orator that Sidney inherited.

1. Docere (to teach/inform the audience); 2. Delectare (to delight/hold attention and win goodwill); 3. Movere (to move/drive to action or change the will).
