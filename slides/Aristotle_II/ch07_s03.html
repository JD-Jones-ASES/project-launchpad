Metaphor and Liveliness (*Energeia*)

Proportional Metaphors

These are the most effective, as they create a "math-like" relationship between two things (e.g., "the spring has been taken out of the year" to describe fallen youth).

Actuality (*Energeia*)

The highest achievement of style is making the audience "see" things in progress. This involves giving life to inanimate objects and representing things in a state of activity.
