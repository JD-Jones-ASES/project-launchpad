Key Concepts

Word Clouds (Tag Clouds)

A visual representation of text data where words are displayed with sizes proportional to their frequency within a given text or dataset. They provide a quick overview of the most prominent terms.

Venn Diagrams

Diagrams that use overlapping circles (or other closed shapes) to illustrate the logical relationships and commonalities between different sets of items.

Important Terms

Word Cloud (Tag Cloud)

A visualization where words from a text or dataset are shown in varying sizes; larger words appear more frequently.

Venn Diagram

A diagram using overlapping circles to show the relationships between different sets, especially commonalities and differences.

Radar Chart (Spider Chart / Web Chart)

A chart displaying multiple quantitative variables as "spokes" radiating from a center point, with values plotted and connected to form a polygon. Used for comparing multi-faceted data.

Gantt Chart

A horizontal bar chart used for project management, illustrating a project schedule with tasks, their durations, and start/end dates over time.

Venn Diagrams

Definition

A Venn diagram uses overlapping circles (or other closed shapes) to visually represent the relationships between two or more distinct sets of items.

Purpose

They illustrate what items belong to specific sets, what items are shared between sets (the intersection), and what items belong to neither set.
