Real-World Applications

Dashboards are widely used in many fields, including weather, sports, health, business, and education, to monitor and analyze data effectively.

Important Terms

Dashboard

A visual display that combines multiple charts, graphs, or data summaries on a single screen or page, allowing a reader to understand many aspects of a topic at once.

Panel

An individual chart, graph, or data summary that makes up a part of a larger dashboard.

Dashboards in the Real World

Weather

Show current temperature, radar, wind speed, and forecasts.

Sports

Display team records, player statistics, and recent game results.

Health

Track disease case counts, hospitalization rates, and vaccination progress (e.g., COVID-19 dashboards).

Business

Monitor sales, costs, profits, and customer metrics in real time.

Schools

Help administrators identify trends in student performance, attendance, and program engagement.
