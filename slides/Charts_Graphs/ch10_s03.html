Important Terms (1/2)

Box Plot

A graphical display that summarizes a dataset using its five-number summary: minimum, first quartile (Q1), median (Q2), third quartile (Q3), and maximum.

Side-by-Side Box Plots

Two or more box plots displayed together on the same numerical axis to facilitate direct comparison of their distributions.

Median

The middle value in an ordered dataset, dividing it into two equal halves. In a box plot, it's the line inside the box.

Interquartile Range (IQR)

The difference between the third quartile (Q3) and the first quartile (Q1), representing the range of the middle 50% of the data. It is the length of the box in a box plot.
