Glossary of Important Terms (1/2)

Aristocracy

Literally "rule by the best" (*aristoi*); a correct form of government in which a virtuous few rule for the benefit of all.

Democracy

In Aristotle's classification, a deviant form of government where the poor majority rule in their own private interest.

Monarchy

Rule by a single virtuous person for the common good; also referred to as a "Kingdom."

Oikos

The Greek household, encompassing family, slaves, and property; treated as the fundamental building block of the city.

Oligarchy

A deviant form of government where a wealthy few rule in their own self-interest.

Glossary of Important Terms (2/2)

Politikon Zoon

Literally "political animal"; refers to the idea that human beings are creatures whose nature is fulfilled only within a city (*polis*).

Polity

Aristotle's term (*politeia*) for a constitutional government where the many rule for the common good; considered the best practically achievable form for most cities.

Tyranny

The worst deviant form of government, characterized by a single person ruling for their own private gain.

Virtue

The ultimate aim of the city's laws and the necessary component of the "good life" that the state is meant to facilitate.
