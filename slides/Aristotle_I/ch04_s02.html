Friendship and the Contemplative Life: A Study Guide

Study Slides

Charity (Caritas) as Friendship

Definition

The theological virtue of love directed toward God, and toward others for God’s sake.

Distinction

Unlike the love of "concupiscence" (desiring something for one's own benefit, like wine or a horse), charity is a love of "benevolence," where one wishes good to the other.

Universality

Charity extends even to enemies because they are loved in relation to God, whom the friend of God loves.

Contemplation and the "Mixed Life"

The Ultimate End

The highest contemplation is the **Beatific Vision**—the direct contemplation of God in heaven, made possible by grace.
