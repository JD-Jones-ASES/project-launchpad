Magnificenza (Magnanimity)

Etymology

Derived from the Latin *magnificentia*, meaning "great-doing."

Philosophical Roots

The concept is tied to Aristotle’s *Nicomachean Ethics*, where *megalopsychia* (magnanimity) is described as the "crown virtue" of a great-souled individual.
