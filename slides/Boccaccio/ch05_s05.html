Plot Summary and Key Entities

Glossary of Important Terms (1/2)

Banderole

A small flag or streamer; in this context, the cloth used by Roussillon to wrap and carry Cabestaing’s heart.

Moral Exemplum

A story told to illustrate a moral point or to serve as a model of behavior, often using extreme scenarios to reveal human truths.

Porringer

A small bowl, often with a handle, used for serving soup or stews (ragouts).

Provençal

Relating to Provence, a region in southern France known for its tradition of troubadours and courtly love.

Ragout

A highly seasoned meat stew; the form in which the heart was served to the lady.

Glossary of Important Terms (2/2)

Troubadour

A poet-musician of southern France (11th–13th centuries) who composed lyrics regarding courtly love.

Vida

A brief prose biography of a troubadour, written in Occitan, which often blended fact with legendary elements.

Yclept

An archaic term meaning "named" or "called."
