Glossary of Important Terms

Key Concepts (1/2)

What is the prescribed theme for Day the Ninth of the Decameron?

There is no prescribed theme (Free Theme).

Which two days of the Decameron allow the storytellers to choose their subjects freely?

Day I and Day IX.

Who is the reigning queen of Day IX?

Emilia.

What is the general tone set by Emilia’s authority on Day IX?

Gentle authority and comic variety.

How does the tone of Day IX differ from the cycles of Day VII and Day VIII?

It is a looser, lighter mode focused on relaxation.

What was the specific narrative theme of Day VII?

Marital trickery.

Key Concepts (2/2)

What was the specific narrative theme of Day VIII?

Elaborate revenge.

How does Day IX modify the concept of the 'beffa' compared to Day VIII?

It pulls the practical joke back from cruelty toward laughter.

What are the common subjects revisited in the tales of Day IX?

Clerical foolishness, bedroom confusion, and practical jokes.

What is the typical structural style of the tales in Day IX?

Brief and almost anecdotal.

What is the primary function of Day IX within the larger structure of the Decameron?

The company 'catches its breath' before the grand finale of Day X.

What external event is still occurring in Florence during the brigata's storytelling?

The plague (pestilence).
