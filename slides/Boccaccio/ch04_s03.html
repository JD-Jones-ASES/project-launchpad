Glossary of Important Terms (1/2)

Abess

The female head of a community of nuns.

Contadino

A peasant or husbandman; a person of the countryside.

Dumb

An archaic term used in the text to describe a person who is unable to speak (mute).

Faggots

Bundles of sticks or branches used for fuel.

Ingegno

A specialized Italian term for wit, genius, or cleverness; the ability to perceive and seize an opportunity.

Glossary of Important Terms (2/2)

Lopping

The act of trimming or cutting branches off a tree.

Prepotent

Having great power or influence; in this context, the overwhelming nature of human urges.

Steward

An official who manages the affairs of an estate or institution; the position Masetto eventually holds at the convent.

Sorely Tasked

To be heavily burdened or strained by a particular duty or expectation.
