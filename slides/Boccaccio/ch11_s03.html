Glossary of Important Terms (1/2)

Brigata

The group of ten young Florentines (seven women, three men) who flee the plague and tell stories to pass the time.

Certes

An archaic term meaning "certainly" or "assuredly."

Dissemble

To conceal one's true feelings, motives, or beliefs; to pretend. (e.g., Griselda dissembling her suffering under a resolute front).

Guerdon

A reward or recompense.

Mien

A person's look or manner, especially one of a particular kind indicating their character or mood.

Glossary of Important Terms (2/2)

Palfrey

A docile horse, especially one well-suited for a lady to ride.

Romagnole

A type of coarse, cheap cloth often worn by peasants.

Shift

A simple, basic garment or undergarment; in the story, it represents the minimum state of dress.

Tierce

A canonical hour of prayer, roughly 9:00 AM.

Vassals

Holders of land by feudal tenure on conditions of homage and allegiance; in the story, they are Gualtieri's subjects who pressure him to marry.
