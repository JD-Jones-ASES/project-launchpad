Important Terms (1/2)

Telos

A Greek word meaning "end," "goal," or "purpose." Cassian uses it for the final destination of the monastic life: the kingdom of heaven.

Scopos

A Greek word meaning "aim" or "target." Cassian uses it for the intermediate goal the monk pursues day by day: purity of heart.

Puritas Cordis

Latin for "purity of heart." Cassian's term for the state of the soul freed from disordered passions and vices, enabling contemplation of God.
