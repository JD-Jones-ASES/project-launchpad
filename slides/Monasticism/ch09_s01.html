Key Concepts

The Enduring Eastern Tradition

Monasticism originated in the East and has continuously flourished there, maintaining ancient practices and adapting to modern challenges, despite often being less known in the Western world.

Mount Athos as a Unique Monastic Republic

A self-governing peninsula in Greece, Mount Athos represents an unbroken, thousand-year-old tradition of monastic prayer, embodying the ancient vision of a life wholly devoted to God.
