Important Terms

Conversion Factor

A ratio of two equivalent measurements that equals 1. Used to convert between different units.

Similar Figures

Figures that have the same shape, where corresponding angles are equal and corresponding sides are proportional.

Proportion

An equation stating that two ratios are equal (e.g., `a/b = c/d`).

Ratio

A comparison of two quantities by division (e.g., `a:b` or `a/b`).

Percent

A ratio out of one hundred. Represented by the symbol `%`.

Summary of Proportional Reasoning Applications

Unit Conversion

Use a conversion factor (a ratio equal to 1) to change units.

Recipe Scaling

Set up a proportion between ingredients and servings to adjust recipe quantities.

Similar Figures

Recognize that corresponding angles are equal and corresponding sides are proportional. Use proportions to find unknown side lengths.

Percent Problems

Translate into `part/whole = percent/100` to solve for any unknown.

Multi-step Problems

Break down into smaller, manageable steps, solving each in order and verifying the final answer.
