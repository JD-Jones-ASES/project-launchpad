Finding a Unit Rate through Division

To calculate a unit rate, you divide both quantities in the rate by the value of the second quantity (the denominator). This process transforms the denominator into 1.

The Meaning of "Per"

The word "per" often indicates a unit rate, meaning "for each" or "for every 1." For example, "miles per hour" means miles for every 1 hour.

Important Terms

Key Concepts (1/2)

What is a 'rate' in the context of ratios?

A rate is a ratio that compares two quantities measured in different units.

Provide an example of a rate.

Examples include $120$ miles in $2$ hours, or $$7.50$ for $3$ pounds. (Any comparison of quantities with different units).

Define a 'unit rate'.

A unit rate is a rate in which the second quantity (the denominator) is $1$.

What is the primary benefit of using unit rates?

Unit rates make it easy to compare prices, speeds, and other everyday measurements by standardizing the comparison to 'per 1' unit.

How do you calculate a unit rate from a given rate?

To find a unit rate, divide both quantities of the rate by the denominator quantity so that the second quantity becomes $1$.

Express the general formula for finding a unit rate from a rate 'a units / b units'.

$\frac{a \text{ units}}{b \text{ units}} = \frac{a/b}{1}$ (where the denominator is reduced to 1).

Key Concepts (2/2)

What does the word 'per' signify in phrases like 'miles per hour' or 'price per item'?

'Per' means 'for each' or 'for every $1$', indicating that you are looking at a unit rate.

A pack of $8$ juice boxes costs $$6.00$. What is the unit price (cost per box)?

The unit price is $$0.75$ per box. (Calculation: $$6.00 \div 8 = $$0.75$)

A car travels $195$ miles in $3$ hours. What is the car's speed as a unit rate?

The car's speed is $65$ miles per hour (mph). (Calculation: $195$ miles \div $3$ hours = $65$ mph)

Why are unit rates particularly useful when determining the 'better buy' between two products?

Unit rates allow for a direct comparison of cost per single item or unit, making it clear which option offers more value for the money.

Brand A sells $12$ pencils for $$3.60$. Brand B sells $8$ pencils for $$2.80$. Which is the better buy?

Brand A is the better buy. (Brand A: $$3.60 \div 12 = $$0.30$ per pencil; Brand B: $$2.80 \div 8 = $$0.35$ per pencil)
