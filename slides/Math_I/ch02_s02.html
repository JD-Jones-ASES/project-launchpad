Important Terms

Understanding Opposites

Definition

Two numbers are opposites if they are equally distant from zero but in opposite directions.

Notation

The opposite of a number `a` is denoted by `-a`.

Key Idea

A number and its opposite essentially "cancel each other out" if added together, resulting in zero (e.g., 7 + (-7) = 0).
