Always verify your division by multiplying the quotient by the divisor. The result should equal the original dividend.

Annexing Zeros

Sometimes, when dividing, you might need to add zeros to the end of the dividend (after the decimal point) to continue the division process and get a more precise quotient.

Key Concepts (1/2)

What is the primary goal when dividing with decimals using long division?

The key idea is to make the divisor a whole number by moving the decimal point.

Define 'Quotient' in a division problem.

The quotient is the result of dividing one number by another. For example, in 6.4 ÷ 2 = 3.2, the quotient is 3.2.

Define 'Dividend' in a division problem.

The dividend is the number being divided. For example, in 6.4 ÷ 2, the dividend is 6.4.

Define 'Divisor' in a division problem.

The divisor is the number you divide by. For example, in 6.4 ÷ 2, the divisor is 2.

What is the first step when dividing a decimal by a whole number?

Place the decimal point in the quotient directly above the decimal point in the dividend.

After placing the decimal point, how do you proceed when dividing a decimal by a whole number?

Divide as with whole numbers. Annex zeros in the dividend if needed to continue dividing.

Key Concepts (2/2)

How do you divide 14.76 by 4?

Place the decimal point in the quotient above the decimal point in 14.76, then divide: 14.76 ÷ 4 = 3.69.

What is the first step when dividing by a decimal?

Move the decimal point in the divisor to the right until the divisor is a whole number.

After making the divisor a whole number, what is the next step when dividing by a decimal?

Move the decimal point in the dividend the same number of places to the right.

What is the final step after adjusting decimal points when dividing by a decimal?

Place the decimal point in the quotient and divide as usual.

How do you divide 7.56 by 0.6?

Move the decimal point one place to the right in both numbers, making it 75.6 ÷ 6. Then divide to get 12.6.

What is 3.225 ÷ 0.25?

Move the decimal point two places to the right in both numbers, making it 322.5 ÷ 25. The answer is 12.9.
