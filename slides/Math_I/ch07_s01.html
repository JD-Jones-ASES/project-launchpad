Key Concepts

Definition of Percent

A percent is a specific type of ratio that expresses a number as a fraction of 100. The term "percent" comes from the Latin "per centum," meaning "per hundred."

The Percent Symbol (%)

The symbol `%` is universally used to denote "out of 100." For example, `45%` directly translates to `45 out of 100` or the fraction `45/100`.

Percent Grid Model

A 10x10 grid, containing 100 small, equal squares, is a visual tool to represent percents. Each individual small square within the grid represents `1%` of the whole.

Key Concepts (1/2)

What is the definition of a percent?

A percent is a ratio that compares a number to 100. The symbol % means 'out of 100'.

From what Latin phrase does the word "percent" originate, and what does it mean?

The word "percent" comes from "per centum," meaning "per hundred."

How would you express 45% as a fraction?

45/100 (or "45 out of 100").

How is a percent grid structured, and what does each small square represent?

A percent grid is a 10x10 square divided into 100 equal small squares. Each small square represents 1%.

On a percent grid, how many squares would you shade to represent 100%?

All 100 small squares.

What does shading 0 squares on a percent grid represent?

Shading 0 squares on a percent grid represents 0%.
