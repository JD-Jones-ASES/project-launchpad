Understanding the Root "cogn, gnosc / gnos"

Study Slides

Key Concepts

Core Meaning

The primary meaning of these roots is "to know" or "to recognize," forming the basis of many English words related to understanding, perception, and awareness.

Dual Origin

The roots derive from both Latin (`cognoscere`) and Greek (`gignoskein`), highlighting a common linguistic ancestry for concepts of knowing across these ancient languages.

Root Forms

The main forms are `cogn` (primarily from Latin) and `gnosc` / `gnos` (from Greek, with `gnos` also appearing as a direct variant from Greek nouns).
