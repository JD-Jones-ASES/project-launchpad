Important Terms (1/2)

Epigraph

A short quotation or saying at the beginning of a book, chapter, or section, intended to suggest its theme.

Sentire

(Latin verb) To perceive, to feel, to sense, to become aware of, to think.

Pati

(Latin verb) To suffer, to endure, to undergo, to experience, to be affected or acted upon.

Sentire est pati

(Latin phrase) "To perceive is to be affected" or "To sense is to suffer/endure."
