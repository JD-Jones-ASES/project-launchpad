Key Concepts (1/2)

What is the procedure used to check if a numerical value is the correct solution to an equation?

Substitute the value back into the original equation to see if both sides are equal.

To solve the equation $x + 7 = 15$, which inverse operation must be applied to both sides?

Subtraction of $7$ from both sides.

In the equation $n - 11 = 7$, what action is required to isolate the variable $n$?

Addition of $11$ to both sides.

What operation is necessary to solve for $x$ in the equation $3x = 21$?

Division of both sides by $3$.

How is the variable $x$ isolated in the one-step equation $\frac{x}{4} = 7$?

Multiplication of both sides by $4$.

In a two-step equation like $2x + 5 = 17$, which operation is typically performed first to isolate the variable term?

Subtraction of the constant $5$ from both sides.

Key Concepts (2/2)

When solving $3n - 7 = 14$, what is the first step in the process of isolation?

Addition of $7$ to both sides of the equation.

To solve $\frac{x}{3} + 4 = 9$, what initial step must be taken before addressing the fraction?

Subtraction of $4$ from both sides.

Which mathematical property is used to simplify the expression $2(x + 4)$ in an equation?

The Distributive Property.

What is the result of applying the distributive property to the expression $-2(3x + 4)$?

$-6x - 8$.

In the equation $4x + 3x = 35$, what is the first step required to solve for $x$?

Combine the like terms on the left side to get $7x$.

What describes the relationship between 'consecutive integers' like $n$ and its successor?

They are integers that follow each other in order, represented as $n$ and $n + 1$.

Quiz Question 1

Solve the following equation for $x$: $$\frac{3}{4}x - 5 = \frac{1}{2}x + 2$$

A. $x = 28$

B. $x = 12$

C. $x = 7$

D. $x = 2.8$

Hint: Try to group all terms with $x$ on one side and all constant numbers on the other side by using inverse operations.

Quiz Question 2

The sum of three consecutive odd integers is $105$. What is the value of the largest integer?

A. $37$

B. $33$

C. $35$

D. $39$

Hint: Represent the three integers as $n$, $n+2$, and $n+4$, then solve for $n$ and find the highest value.

Quiz Question 3

Solve the literal equation for the variable $x$: $$y = mx + b$$

A. $x = \frac{y - b}{m}$

B. $x = \frac{y + b}{m}$

C. $x = m(y - b)$

D. $x = \frac{m}{y - b}$

Hint: Isolate the term containing $x$ by removing the constant $b$ first, then address the coefficient $m$.

Quiz Question 4

Solve the proportion for $x$: $$\frac{x + 5}{4} = \frac{x - 2}{3}$$

A. $x = 23$

B. $x = 7$

C. $x = 13$

D. $x = -23$

Hint: Use the cross-product property to turn the proportion into a linear equation.

Quiz Question 5

A smartphone is on sale for $25\%$ off. If the sale price is $\$450$, what was the original price of the phone?

A. $\$600$

B. $\$562.50$

C. $\$500$

D. $\$337.50$

Hint: If the discount is $25\%$, the price you pay is $75\%$ of the original amount.

Quiz Question 6

Identify the solution to the equation: $$5(2x - 4) = 10x - 20$$

A. All real numbers

B. No solution

C. $x = 0$

D. $x = 4$

Hint: Distribute the $5$ on the left side and compare the resulting expression to the right side.
