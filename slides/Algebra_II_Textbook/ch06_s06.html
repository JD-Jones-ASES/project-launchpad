Important Terms

Rational Equation

An equation that includes at least one rational expression (a fraction where the numerator and/or denominator contains a variable).

Extraneous Solution

A solution derived from solving a transformed equation that does not satisfy the original equation because it makes a denominator equal to zero.

Least Common Denominator (LCD)

The smallest multiple that all denominators in a set of rational expressions divide into evenly. Used to clear denominators in rational equations.

Work Rate

The fraction of a job that can be completed per unit of time. If a job takes $t$ hours to complete, the work rate is $1/t$ jobs per hour.
