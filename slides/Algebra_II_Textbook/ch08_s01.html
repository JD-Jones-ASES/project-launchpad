Transformations I: Shifts and Reflections

Study Slides

Key Concepts

Important Terms (1/2)

Parent Function

The most basic function of a type or family, from which other functions in the family can be derived through transformations.

Graphical Transformations

Arithmetic operations applied to a function's inputs or outputs that change the position or orientation of its graph, such as shifts and reflections.

Vertical Shift

A transformation that moves a graph up or down by adding or subtracting a constant to the function's output.

Horizontal Shift

A transformation that moves a graph left or right by adding or subtracting a constant to the function's input.

Reflection over the x-axis

A transformation that flips a graph across the horizontal x-axis, changing the sign of the y-coordinates of all points.

Important Terms (2/2)

Reflection over the y-axis

A transformation that flips a graph across the vertical y-axis, changing the sign of the x-coordinates of all points.
