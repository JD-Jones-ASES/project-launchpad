Business Applications - Percents in Business

Study Slides

Key Concepts (1/2)

Sales Tax

A percentage added to the purchase price of goods or services, collected by sellers on behalf of the government, resulting in a total price higher than the tag price.

Tipping/Gratuity

A voluntary payment, typically a percentage of the pre-tax bill, given to service staff as an appreciation for their service.

Commissions

A form of payment where an individual's earnings are based directly on the amount or value of sales they make, often a percentage of total sales.

Year-Over-Year (YOY) Growth

A critical metric used by businesses to compare their performance (e.g., sales, revenue, expenses) for the same period in two consecutive years, indicating growth or decline.

Successive Discounts

Multiple discounts applied sequentially to an item, where each subsequent discount is calculated on the already reduced price, not the original price.

Key Concepts (2/2)

Finding Original Price

The process of calculating the initial cost of an item before any discounts were applied, given the discounted price and the discount rate.

Important Terms (1/2)

Sales Tax

A percentage of a purchase price added by the store, collected for state or local government.

Tip (Gratuity)

An additional payment made to service staff, usually a percentage of the pre-tax bill.

Commission

Earnings based on the volume or value of sales made, expressed as a percentage of total sales.

Commission Rate

The percentage used to calculate the commission earned on sales.

Year-Over-Year (YOY) Growth

A measure of change in a financial metric (like revenue) from one year to the next, comparing the same period.

Important Terms (2/2)

Successive Discounts

A series of discounts applied sequentially, with each discount taken from the current reduced price.

Calculating Sales Tax

Definition

A percentage of the purchase price that goes to the state or local government. The price on the tag is typically *before* tax.

Total Cost

The amount you pay is the original price plus the sales tax.

Calculation Steps

One-Step Method for Total Price

You can find the total price including tax by multiplying the original price by `(1 + tax rate)`.

Figuring Tips (Gratuity)

Definition

An additional amount paid by a customer for a service, typically a percentage of the bill. Also known as gratuity.

Common Rates

Usually range from $15%$ to $20%$.

Basis for Tip

In the U.S., tips are generally calculated on the *pre-tax amount* of the bill.

Concept

If an item is $20%$ off, the customer pays $100% - 20% = 80%$ of the original price.
