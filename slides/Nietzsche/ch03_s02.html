Quiz Question 2

Which of the following best describes the primary divergence between Hegel's and Nietzsche's views on the master-slave dynamic?

A. Hegel views the relationship as a progressive path to reconciliation, while Nietzsche sees it as an ongoing struggle without a happy synthesis.

B. Hegel sides with the master's vitality, whereas Nietzsche sides with the slave's creative power.

C. Nietzsche believes the master-slave relationship is a historical necessity, while Hegel believes it is a modern construct.

D. Hegel focuses on the material interests of the classes, while Nietzsche focuses on the spiritual growth of the individual.

Hint: Think about which philosopher believes in a 'happy ending' of mutual recognition and progress.

Quiz Question 3

D. It was a movement led by the weak that resulted in the most powerful ruling class in history.

Quiz Question 6
