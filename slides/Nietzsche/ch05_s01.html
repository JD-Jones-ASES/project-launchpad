The Will to Nothingness

Redirection vs. Extinction

Unlike Schopenhauer, who believed the will could be quieted through asceticism or contemplation, Nietzsche argued the will cannot be extinguished.

The Role of the Ascetic Priest

Ingenuity of the Weak

The priest represents the creativity of the weak, who must be more ingenious than the strong to survive and lead.

Reinterpretation as Treatment

The priest "treats" suffering by reinterpreting it as guilt. By telling the sufferer they are responsible for their own pain, the priest makes the pain bearable by giving it a moral framework.
