Philosophical Asceticism

The "Desert"

Philosophers seek a "hermitage" or "deliberate obscurity" to avoid the distractions of "today," such as politics, news, and "democratic babble."

Function

Self-denial provides the "freedom from compulsion" and the "clear head" required for deep thought.
