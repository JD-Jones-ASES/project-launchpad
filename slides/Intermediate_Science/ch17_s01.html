Important Terms

Continental shelf

The gently sloping, shallow underwater extension of a continent, extending from the shoreline to the shelf break.

Abyssal plain

The vast, flat region of the deep ocean floor, typically 3000–6000m below the surface, covered by thick sediment.

Salinity

The total amount of dissolved salts in water, typically expressed in parts per thousand (ppt) or grams of salt per kilogram of water.

Thermocline

The layer of ocean water in which temperature decreases rapidly with increasing depth, separating warm surface water from cold deep water.

Evaporation
