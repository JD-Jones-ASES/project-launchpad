Characteristics of Action-Reaction Pairs

Why Action-Reaction Forces Do Not Cancel

Because action and reaction forces act on *different objects*, they cannot cancel each other out. Forces can only cancel if they act on the *same* object.

Action-Reaction Pair

Two forces that are equal in magnitude, opposite in direction, and act on two different objects, as described by Newton's Third Law.

Normal Force

A contact force perpendicular to the surface that prevents an object from penetrating the surface. It is a distinct force and not the reaction to gravity acting on an object resting on a surface.

Friction

A force that opposes motion between two surfaces in contact. It is the reaction force from the ground that propels you forward when walking.
