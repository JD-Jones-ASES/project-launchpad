How Atoms Achieve an Octet: Three Types of Bonds

Very different electronegativities

Leads to electron *transfer* and **ionic bonds**.

Similar electronegativities

Leads to electron *sharing* and **covalent bonds**.

Low electronegativities (all metals)

Leads to electron *pooling* and **metallic bonds**.
