What is the main purpose of this 'Vocabulary Review' section?

To collect key vocabulary terms from all four units in Cycle 2 to serve as a study guide and self-check.

Key Concepts (2/2)

What are two fundamental ways suggested for students to review each vocabulary term?

To define each term in their own words and to give an example for each term.

Why is it important to define vocabulary terms in your 'own words' rather than just memorizing a definition?

Defining terms in your own words demonstrates a deeper understanding and comprehension of the concept, rather than rote memorization.

What is the benefit of providing an example for each vocabulary term during review?

Providing an example helps to solidify understanding by illustrating how the term applies in a practical, real-world, or specific context.

What overarching theme is suggested for connecting vocabulary terms from different scientific branches?

The theme suggested for connection is 'structure determines function'.

How does the 'structure determines function' theme encourage deeper analytical thinking when connecting terms across branches?

What is the objective of the 'thinkdeeper' activity described in this section?

To choose one term from each of the four branches and write a single paragraph that uses all four terms together in a meaningful way.
