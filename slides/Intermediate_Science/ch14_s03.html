Mechanical Energy

The sum of the kinetic energy ($KE$) and potential energy ($PE$) in a system, expressed as $E = KE + PE$.

Isolated System

A system that does not exchange matter or energy with its surroundings. The total energy within such a system remains constant.

Efficiency

The ratio of useful energy output to total energy input, expressed as a percentage. It quantifies how much of the input energy is converted into the desired form of output energy.
