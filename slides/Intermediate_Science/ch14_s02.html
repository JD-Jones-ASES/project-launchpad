Key Concepts (1/2)

Energy as the Ability to Cause Change

Energy is the fundamental capacity to do work or cause a change in an object or system.

Energy and Work Relationship

Doing work on an object transfers energy to it. Energy is measured in joules (J), the same unit as work, and is a scalar quantity (has magnitude but no direction).

Gravitational Potential Energy (GPE) (1/2)

Definition

Gravitational potential energy is the energy stored in an object because of its height above a reference point.

How it's Stored

When you lift an object, you do work against gravity. This work is stored as gravitational potential energy.

Conversion

If the object is allowed to fall, this stored potential energy converts back into kinetic energy.

Conversion

When released, this stored elastic energy converts into kinetic energy.
