The Scope of the Review

Life Science — Unit 1: The Cell

Focus on terms related to cellular biology, functions, structures, and processes relevant to living organisms.

Earth Science — Unit 2: Earth's Dynamic Interior

Review terms associated with geology, plate tectonics, Earth's layers, and internal processes.

Chemistry — Unit 3: Inside the Atom

Concentrate on atomic structure, subatomic particles, basic chemical bonding, and elements.

Physics — Unit 4: Describing Motion

Master terms related to kinematics, forces, speed, velocity, acceleration, and Newton's Laws.
