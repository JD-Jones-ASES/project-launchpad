Key Concepts (1/2)

What is Amara's Law?

We tend to overestimate the effect of a technology in the short run and underestimate its effect in the long run.

Who is Roy Amara, to whom Amara's Law is attributed?

A researcher and president of the Institute for the Future, a Silicon Valley think tank, known for technology forecasting.
