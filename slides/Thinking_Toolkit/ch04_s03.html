Everyday Utility

This heuristic works reasonably well in many daily situations. If an animal looks like a duck, walks like a duck, and quacks like a duck, it's highly probable that it is, in fact, a duck.

Predictable Failures

The problem arises when resemblance and actual probability diverge. The heuristic ignores crucial statistical information, particularly **base rates**.
