Implications: Warning and Opportunity

The Warning

Our own lives (jobs, relationships, routines) might be suboptimal but persist due to status quo bias. We may not have mustered the "activation energy" required for change.

The Opportunity

If designing systems for others (e.g., retirement plans, health programs, software), one can harness the default effect to encourage beneficial outcomes by making the desirable option the default.

Concept

Coined by Richard Thaler and Cass Sunstein in their book *Nudge* (2008).
