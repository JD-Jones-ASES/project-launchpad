Cautions and Nuances

Approximation, Not a Constant

Dunbar's Number is an approximation, not a precise, rigid constant. There is significant individual variation; some people can maintain more relationships, others fewer.

Specific Type of Relationship

The "150" figure refers to a specific type of relationship—individuals you know well enough to have a genuine, casual interaction with, like "sitting down uninvited for a drink."
