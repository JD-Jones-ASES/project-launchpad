Important Terms (1/2)

Streetlight Effect

Drunkard's Search

An informal name for the Streetlight Effect, derived from the joke illustrating the bias.

Observational Bias

An academic term for the Streetlight Effect, particularly in research, where researchers gravitate towards problems amenable to their existing methods, data, or instruments.

Cognitive Bias

A systematic error in thinking that affects the decisions and judgments that people make. The Streetlight Effect is presented as one such bias.

Goodhart's Law

A related principle (mentioned in connections) stating that "When a measure becomes a target, it ceases to be a good measure." This highlights the dangers of over-relying on metrics.
