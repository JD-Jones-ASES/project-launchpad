The Quadratic Formula

Study Slides

Application Procedure

Rearrange into Standard Form

Ensure the equation is written as $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$.

Identify Coefficients

Determine the values for $a$ (the coefficient of $x^2$), $b$ (the coefficient of $x$), and $c$ (the constant term).

Substitute and Simplify

Plug the values into the formula and simplify the arithmetic and radicals.
