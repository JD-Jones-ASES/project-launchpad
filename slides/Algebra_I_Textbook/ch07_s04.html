Glossary of Important Terms

Key Concepts (1/2)

What is the primary benefit of recognizing special polynomial forms during factoring?

They allow for quick factoring without the need to list factor pairs.

What is the factoring pattern for the difference of squares $a^2 - b^2$?

$(a + b)(a - b)$

To use the difference of squares pattern, the expression must involve which mathematical operation?

Subtraction

To apply the pattern $a^2 - b^2$, both terms in the expression must be categorized as      .

perfect squares

In the factoring example $x^2 - 49$, what is the value of $a$?

$x$

In the factoring example $x^2 - 49$, what is the value of $b$?

$7$

Key Concepts (2/2)

Factor the expression $x^2 - 49$.

$(x + 7)(x - 7)$

What is the result of checking the factors $(x + 7)(x - 7)$ using multiplication?

$x^2 - 49$

Express the term $4y^2$ as a perfect square $(a)^2$.

$(2y)^2$

In the expression $4y^2 - 25$, what is the value of $b$ for the difference of squares pattern?

$5$

Factor the expression $4y^2 - 25$.

$(2y + 5)(2y - 5)$

What is the value of the middle terms when checking $(2y + 5)(2y - 5)$ with FOIL?

$-10y$ and $+10y$ (which sum to zero)
