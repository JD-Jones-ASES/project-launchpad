Multiplying Polynomials

Study Slides

Monomial and Polynomial

Expression

$-3x^2(4x^3 - 2x + 5)$

Distribution

$(-3x^2)(4x^3) + (-3x^2)(-2x) + (-3x^2)(5)$

Result

$-12x^5 + 6x^3 - 15x^2$

Two Binomials (FOIL Method)

Letter. Meaning

Description

Multiply the first terms of each binomial.

O. Outer

Multiply the outermost terms of the expression.

Multiply the innermost terms of the expression.

L. Last

Multiply the last terms of each binomial.

Glossary of Important Terms

Key Concepts (1/2)

While addition and subtraction of polynomials involve combining like terms, what technique is used for multiplying polynomials?

The distributive property.

To multiply a monomial by a polynomial, you must distribute the monomial to _____ and then simplify.

each term of the polynomial

Formula: The Distributive Property for a monomial $m$ and polynomial $p_1 + p_2 + \dots + p_k$.

$m(p_1 + p_2 + \dots + p_k) = mp_1 + mp_2 + \dots + mp_k$

What is the first step in multiplying $-3x^2(4x^3 - 2x + 5)$?

Distribute $-3x^2$ to each term in the parentheses.

In the expression $-3x^2(4x^3 - 2x + 5)$, what is the result of multiplying the monomial by the first term $4x^3$?

$-12x^5$

In the expression $-3x^2(4x^3 - 2x + 5)$, what is the result of multiplying the monomial by the second term $-2x$?

$6x^3$

Key Concepts (2/2)

In the expression $-3x^2(4x^3 - 2x + 5)$, what is the result of multiplying the monomial by the constant term $5$?

$-15x^2$

What is the simplified product of $-3x^2(4x^3 - 2x + 5)$?

$-12x^5 + 6x^3 - 15x^2$

To multiply two binomials, the distributive property must be applied how many times?

Twice.

What mnemonic is used to remember the steps for multiplying two binomials?

FOIL

In the FOIL mnemonic, what does 'F' represent?

First

In the FOIL mnemonic, what does 'O' represent?

Outer
