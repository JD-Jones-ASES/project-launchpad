Short-Answer Practice Questions (1/2)

Q: What is the fundamental definition of a ratio?

> Answer: A ratio is a comparison of two quantities by division.

Q: Solve for $x$ in the following proportion: $\frac{x}{6} = \frac{5}{9}$.

> Answer: Cross-multiply to get $9x = 30$. Solving for $x$ results in $x = \frac{30}{9}$, which simplifies to $\frac{10}{3}$.

Q: If a proportion is set up as $\frac{3}{n} = \frac{12}{20}$, what is the value of $n$?

> Answer: Cross-multiply to get $3 \times 20 = 12n$, which is $60 = 12n$. Dividing by 12 gives $n = 5$.

Short-Answer Practice Questions (2/2)

Q: Solve the following checkpoint problem: $\frac{4}{7} = \frac{x}{21}$.

> Answer: Cross-multiply to get $4 \times 21 = 7x$, resulting in $84 = 7x$. Therefore, $x = 12$.

Q: Two similar triangles have corresponding sides in a ratio of $3:5$. If the shortest side of the smaller triangle is $9\text{ cm}$, what is the length of the corresponding side of the larger triangle?

> Answer: Set up the proportion $\frac{3}{5} = \frac{9}{x}$. Cross-multiply to get $3x = 45$. The length of the corresponding side is $15\text{ cm}$.

Glossary of Important Terms

Key Concepts (1/2)

What is the definition of a ratio?

A comparison of two quantities by division.

In the ratio of $a$ to $b$, what is the mathematical constraint on the value of $b$?

$b eq 0$

The ratio of $a$ to $b$ can be written using a colon as _____.

$a : b$

The ratio of $a$ to $b$ can be written as a fraction in the form _____.

$\frac{a}{b}$

Besides using a colon or a fraction, what is the third way to write the ratio of $a$ to $b$?

$a$ to $b$

What is a proportion?

An equation stating that two ratios are equal.

Key Concepts (2/2)

In the proportion $\frac{a}{b} = \frac{c}{d}$, which values are prohibited from being zero?

$b$ and $d$

What is considered the most efficient way to solve a proportion?

Cross-multiplication.

State the Cross-Multiplication Property for the proportion $\frac{a}{b} = \frac{c}{d}$.

$ad = bc$

Solve the proportion $\frac{x}{6} = \frac{5}{9}$ by showing the first step of cross-multiplication.

$9x = 30$

What is the simplified solution for $x$ in the proportion $\frac{x}{6} = \frac{5}{9}$?

$x = \frac{10}{3}$

Solve the proportion $\frac{3}{n} = \frac{12}{20}$ by showing the first step of cross-multiplication.

$60 = 12n$
