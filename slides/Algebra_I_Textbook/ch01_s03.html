Core Properties of Real Numbers

Property. Addition

Multiplication

Commutative Property. $a + b = b + a$

$ab = ba$

Associative Property. $(a + b) + c = a + (b + c)$

$(ab)c = a(bc)$

Identity Property. $a + 0 = a$ (Identity: $0$)

$a \cdot 1 = a$ (Identity: $1$)

Inverse Property. $a + (-a) = 0$ (Inverse: $-a$)

$a \cdot \frac{1}{a} = 1, a eq 0$ (Inverse: $\frac{1}{a}$)

The Distributive Property

Addition

$a(b + c) = ab + ac$

Subtraction

$a(b - c) = ab - ac$

Non-Commutative Operations

Subtraction

$8 - 3 = 5$, whereas $3 - 8 = -5$.

Division

$12 \div 4 = 3$, whereas $4 \div 12 = \frac{1}{3}$.

Glossary of Important Terms (1/2)

Additive Identity

The number $0$, which, when added to any real number, results in the original number.

Additive Inverse

Also known as the opposite; the number $-a$ which, when added to $a$, equals $0$.

Associative Property

A rule stating that the way in which numbers are grouped in addition or multiplication does not change their sum or product.

Commutative Property

A rule stating that the order in which numbers are added or multiplied does not change their sum or product.

Distributive Property

A property that allows a multiplier outside of parentheses to be distributed to each term within the parentheses.

Glossary of Important Terms (2/2)

Multiplicative Identity

The number $1$, which, when multiplied by any real number, results in the original number.

Multiplicative Inverse

Also known as the reciprocal; the number $\frac{1}{a}$ (where $a eq 0$) which, when multiplied by $a$, equals $1$.

Opposite

The additive inverse of a number.

Reciprocal

The multiplicative inverse of a number.

Key Concepts (1/2)

According to the Commutative Property of Addition, $a + b$ is equal to which expression?

$b + a$

What is the algebraic identity for the Commutative Property of Multiplication?

$ab = ba$

Which property states that changing the order of two numbers being added does not change the sum?

Commutative Property of Addition

The property illustrating that the order in which two numbers are multiplied does not affect the product is the _____ Property of Multiplication.

Commutative

State the algebraic form of the Associative Property of Addition.

$(a + b) + c = a + (b + c)$

State the algebraic form of the Associative Property of Multiplication.

$(ab)c = a(bc)$

Key Concepts (2/2)

Which property states that changing the grouping of three numbers being added does not change the sum?

Associative Property of Addition

The _____ Property of Multiplication allows for the regrouping of factors without changing the product.

Associative

In the context of the Identity Properties, what is the 'identity' for addition?

$0$

In the context of the Identity Properties, what is the 'identity' for multiplication?

$1$

What is the result of the expression $a + 0$ according to the Additive Identity Property?

$a$

According to the Multiplicative Identity Property, the product of any real number $a$ and $1$ is _____.

$a$
