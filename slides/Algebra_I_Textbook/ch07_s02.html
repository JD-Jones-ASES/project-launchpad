Glossary of Important Terms (1/2)

Binomial

An algebraic expression consisting of two terms, such as $(x + 3)$.

Coefficient

A numerical or constant quantity placed before and multiplying the variable in an algebraic expression (e.g., in $8x$, 8 is the coefficient).

Constant Term

The term in a polynomial that does not contain a variable (the $c$ in $x^2 + bx + c$).

Factoring

The process of breaking down a mathematical expression into a product of simpler expressions.

FOIL Method

A mnemonic for multiplying two binomials: First, Outer, Inner, Last.

Glossary of Important Terms (2/2)

Leading Coefficient

The coefficient of the term with the highest exponent. In this section, the leading coefficient of $x^2$ is 1.

Prime (Irreducible)

A polynomial that cannot be factored into polynomials of lower degree with integer coefficients.

Trinomial

An algebraic expression consisting of three terms, such as $x^2 + 8x + 15$.

Quiz Question 1

To factor the trinomial $x^2 + bx + c$, which of the following conditions must the integers $p$ and $q$ satisfy?

A. $p + q = b$ and $pq = c$

B. $pq = b$ and $p + q = c$

C. $p - q = b$ and $pq = c$

D. $p + q = -b$ and $pq = c$

Hint: Consider the FOIL method and how the middle term is formed by adding the inner and outer products.

Quiz Question 2

Which of the following describes the signs of $p$ and $q$ when factoring $x^2 - 13x + 36$?

A. Both $p$ and $q$ must be negative.

B. Both $p$ and $q$ must be positive.

C. The signs of $p$ and $q$ must be different.

D. The signs depend on which integer has the larger absolute value.

Hint: Look at the signs of both the constant term and the coefficient of the middle term.

Quiz Question 3

Factor the trinomial: $x^2 - 13x + 42$.

A. $(x - 6)(x - 7)$

B. $(x - 3)(x - 14)$

C. $(x + 6)(x + 7)$

D. $(x - 21)(x - 2)$

Hint: Search for two negative factors of $42$ that sum to $-13$.

Quiz Question 4

What is the factored form of $x^2 + 4x - 60$?

A. $(x + 10)(x - 6)$

B. $(x - 10)(x + 6)$

C. $(x + 12)(x - 5)$

D. $(x + 15)(x - 4)$

Hint: Since the constant is negative, find two numbers with different signs whose sum is $4$.

Quiz Question 5

Which of these trinomials is considered 'prime' or irreducible over the integers?

A. $x^2 + 4x + 5$

B. $x^2 - x - 2$

C. $x^2 + 5x + 6$

D. $x^2 - 6x + 9$

Hint: A trinomial is prime if no pair of integers exists that satisfies both the sum and product requirements.

Quiz Question 6

Factor the trinomial: $x^2 - 10x - 24$.

A. $(x - 12)(x + 2)$

B. $(x - 6)(x - 4)$

C. $(x - 8)(x - 3)$

D. $(x + 12)(x - 2)$

Hint: Ensure your pair of factors results in a negative product and a sum of $-10$.
