Motion and Speed Study Guide

Study Slides

Key Concepts (1/2)

Motion

Motion is defined as any change in an object's position over a period of time. If an object's location relative to a reference point changes, it is in motion.

Speed

Speed is a measure of how quickly an object is changing its position. It tells us how fast something is moving without considering the direction.

Calculating Speed

Speed is mathematically determined by dividing the total distance an object travels by the total time it takes to travel that distance. The formula is: `Speed = Distance / Time`.

Units of Speed

Speed is typically expressed in units that combine a unit of distance and a unit of time, such as meters per second (m/s) or kilometers per hour (km/h).

Comparing Speeds

An object with a higher speed covers more distance in the same amount of time, or covers the same distance in less time, compared to an object with a lower speed.

Important Terms

Motion

A change in an object's position over time.

Position

The location of an object in space.

Speed

A measure of how fast an object moves; calculated by dividing the distance traveled by the time it took to travel that distance.

Distance

The total length of the path an object travels.

Time

The duration over which an event occurs or is measured.

Key Concepts (1/2)

What is motion?

Motion is a change in an object's position over time.

Give two examples of objects in motion mentioned in the text.

A car driving down the street, or Earth spinning and orbiting the Sun.

How do scientists measure 'how fast' an object moves?

Scientists measure an object's speed.

Define speed.

Speed is how fast an object moves.

What is the formula for calculating speed?

Speed = Distance ÷ Time.

Calculate the speed of someone who walks 20 meters in 10 seconds.

Speed = 20 m / 10 s = 2 meters per second.

Key Concepts (2/2)

What does a speed of '2 meters per second' mean?

It means the object moves 2 meters every second.

A bicycle travels 100 meters in 20 seconds. What is its speed?

Speed = 100 m / 20 s = 5 meters per second.
