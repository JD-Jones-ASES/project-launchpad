What Is Science?

Definition

Science is fundamentally the process of asking questions and finding answers about the natural world.

Everyday Science

You engage in scientific thinking whenever you observe something interesting and try to understand how it works.

Guesses and Hypotheses

Regular Guesses

These are often predictions about future events or personal preferences (e.g., "My favorite team will win," "I think chocolate ice cream is the best"). They are not necessarily based on evidence an...

Hypothesis Defined

A hypothesis is a *testable prediction*. It represents a scientist's best educated guess about what will happen and often includes a reason why.

The Key Difference: Testability

What makes a hypothesis scientific is that it *can be tested*. You can design an experiment or gather evidence to determine if it is right or wrong.
