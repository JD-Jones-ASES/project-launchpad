What Is a Force? - Study Guide

Study Slides

Key Concepts

Definition of Force

A force is fundamentally defined as a push or a pull. This action is what allows a force to interact with an object.

Effect on Motion

The defining characteristic of a force is its ability to change an object's motion. This includes causing it to start moving, stop moving, speed up, slow down, or alter its direction.

Magnitude of Force Matters

Not all forces are equal in strength. The size or "magnitude" of a force directly influences the size of the change in motion it can produce; a larger force generally results in a greater change.

Important Terms

Force

A push or a pull that can change an object's motion by making it start, stop, speed up, slow down, or change direction.

Motion

The action or process of moving or being moved. In physics, motion refers to a change in position over time.

Friction

A force that opposes motion between two surfaces that are in contact and sliding or tending to slide past each other. It's often responsible for slowing down or stopping moving objects.

Defining Force
