Key Concepts

The Body as a Team

Your body is composed of many parts, each with a specific job, but no part works in isolation. Like a sports team or a city, all parts depend on each other for smooth functioning.

Body Systems

Groups of organs that work together to perform a specific, larger job in the body. Examples include the skeletal, muscular, digestive, circulatory, respiratory, and nervous systems.

Important Terms

Body system

A group of organs that work together to do a specific job in the body.

Cell

The smallest living unit in your body. Every part of you is made of cells.

Tissue

A group of similar cells that work together to do the same job.

Organ

A body part made of different tissues that work together to do a specific job.
