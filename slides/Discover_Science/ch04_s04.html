Newton's First Law of Motion (Law of Inertia)

A scientific principle stating that an object will remain at rest or in uniform motion in a straight line unless acted upon by an external force.

How Surface Roughness Affects Friction

Rougher Surfaces = More Friction

When surfaces are rough, they have more irregularities that "catch" or interlock with each other, creating greater resistance to movement.

Smoother Surfaces = Less Friction

Smooth surfaces have fewer irregularities, allowing them to glide past each other with less resistance.
