Important Terms (1/2)

Oral Tradition

The transmission of stories, history, and cultural knowledge from one generation to the next through speech rather than writing.

Creation Myth

A symbolic narrative of how the world began and how people first came to inhabit it, often explaining the origins of natural phenomena and human customs.

Mythology

A collection of myths, especially one belonging to a particular religious or cultural tradition.

Legend

A traditional story sometimes popularly regarded as historical but unauthenticated.

Sacred Texts

Writings that are believed to be divinely inspired or otherwise holy, serving as foundational documents for religions or spiritual practices.
