Deliverance Through Song

The idea that spirituals, in particular, provided a spiritual and emotional pathway to cope with hardship and maintain hope for liberation, both in this life and the next.

Important Terms (1/2)

Psalm

A sacred song or poem, especially one from the biblical Book of Psalms, used in worship and personal devotion.

Hymn

A religious song or poem, typically of praise or adoration for God, sung by a congregation in a church service.

Spiritual

A type of religious folk song, originating among enslaved African people in the United States, characterized by themes of suffering, hope, and the desire for freedom and salvation.

Deliverance

The act of being rescued or set free from danger, suffering, or hardship. In the context of spirituals, this often refers to freedom from slavery or salvation in a spiritual sense.

Suffering

The state of undergoing pain, distress, or hardship, often referring to physical, emotional, or spiritual anguish.

Important Terms (2/2)

Congregational Singing

The act of a group of people, typically a church congregation, singing together as part of a religious service.
