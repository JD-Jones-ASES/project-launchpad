Introduction to Knights and Knaves

The Premise

You are on an island inhabited by two distinct types of people: Knights and Knaves.

The Rule

Knights *always* tell the truth. Knaves *always* lie. There are no exceptions.

The Goal

Your task is to figure out who is a Knight and who is a Knave based purely on the statements they make.
