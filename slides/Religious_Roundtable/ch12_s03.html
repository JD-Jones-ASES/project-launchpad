Species-Being: The Essence of Humanity

Defining Human Nature

The concept of "species-being" (Gattungswesen) is Marx's term for the characteristic activity that defines the human species.

Conscious, Free Production

What distinguishes humans from other animals is our capacity for conscious, free, and purposeful production.

Source of Dignity and Self-Realization

For Marx, work should be a dignified, creative, and self-realizing activity through which we express ourselves and transform the world.
