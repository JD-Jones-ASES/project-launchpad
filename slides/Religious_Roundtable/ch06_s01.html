Yin and Yang

Glossary of Important Terms (1/2)

Tao

Translated as "the Way"; the nameless, formless source and underlying order of all reality. It is the fundamental nature of the universe.

Wu wei

Literally "non-action" or "effortless action." The practice of acting in accordance with the natural flow of things without forcing or imposing one's will.

Ziran

Naturalness or spontaneity. The state of acting from one's true nature without artifice; the result of realized *wu wei*.

Yin and yang

Two complementary, interdependent forces (e.g., dark/light, soft/hard) whose interplay generates all phenomena.

Yin

The force associated with receptivity, darkness, softness, and the feminine.

Glossary of Important Terms (2/2)

Yang

The force associated with activity, light, firmness, and the masculine.
