Key Concepts and Themes

Universal Human Dignity

Derived from the concept of *imago Dei* (the belief that humans are made in the image of God), asserting that every person possesses inherent worth regardless of status.

Natural Rights

Developed by Christian thinkers such as Thomas Aquinas and John Locke, forming the basis for modern democratic governance.
