Glossary of Key Terms (1/2)

Classless Society

The final stage of history in Marxist theory; a vision of ultimate redemption characterized by the end of alienation and the restoration of human wholeness.

Cosmic Story

A comprehensive narrative of reality; in Communism, this is fulfilled by the theory of historical materialism.

Eschatological Hope

A vision concerning the ultimate destiny of humanity or the end of history.

Faith (Tillich’s Definition)

The structure of human commitment itself; whatever a person treats as their "ultimate concern."

Historical Materialism

The theory that history is driven by material conditions (production and labor) rather than ideas.

Glossary of Key Terms (2/2)

Opiate of the Masses

A metaphor used by Marx to describe religion as a substance that numbs people to the pain of their real-world suffering.

The Sacred (Eliade’s Definition)

Any reality that is set apart from ordinary life and treated with special significance.

Solidarity

A moral demand in Communism requiring unity with the oppressed and the subordination of the self to the revolutionary cause.
