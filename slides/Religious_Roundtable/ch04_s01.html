Core Philosophical Concepts (1/3)

The Ground of All Being

The ultimate, infinite, and impersonal reality that underlies and pervades all existence.

The Source and Destination

The origin from which everything arises and the end to which everything eventually returns.

Non-Personal

Limitless

It is without beginning, end, or boundary.

Atman

This refers to the innermost self or individual soul. In deep Hindu philosophy, Atman is not separate from Brahman; it is the universal self "wearing the costume of an individual."
