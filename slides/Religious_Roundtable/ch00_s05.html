Glossary of Key Terms (1/2)

Classless Society

The final stage of history in Marxist theory; a vision of ultimate redemption characterized by the end of alienation and the restoration of human wholeness.

Cosmic Story

A comprehensive narrative of reality; in Communism, this is fulfilled by the theory of historical materialism.

Eschatological Hope

A vision concerning the ultimate destiny of humanity or the end of history.

Faith (Tillich’s Definition)

The structure of human commitment itself; whatever a person treats as their "ultimate concern."

Historical Materialism

The theory that history is driven by material conditions (production and labor) rather than ideas.

Glossary of Key Terms (2/2)

Opiate of the Masses

A metaphor used by Marx to describe religion as a substance that numbs people to the pain of their real-world suffering.

The Sacred (Eliade’s Definition)

Any reality that is set apart from ordinary life and treated with special significance.

Solidarity

A moral demand in Communism requiring unity with the oppressed and the subordination of the self to the revolutionary cause.

He called it 'the opiate of the masses.'

Religion numbed people to their real suffering and prevented them from addressing their issues, much like a drug numbs pain.

Key Concepts (2/2)

According to Mircea Eliade's definition, is 'the sacred' always supernatural?

No, Eliade's definition expands the concept of the sacred beyond just the supernatural to include any reality treated with special significance.

What broad scholarly frameworks justify the analytical classification of Communism as having the 'full structure of a religion'?

What is Communism's 'cosmic story' that explains all of human history?

It is the theory of historical materialism, which explains history as a movement through stages toward a final classless society.

List the stages of human history according to the theory of historical materialism.

Primitive communism, slavery, feudalism, capitalism, and finally, a classless society.

How does Communism's theory of historical materialism function similarly to a religious creation myth?

It provides a comprehensive explanation of reality and human history, offering a grand narrative akin to how creation myths explain origins and destiny.

What are some 'moral demands' that Communism places on its adherents?

Calls for solidarity with the oppressed, resistance to exploitation, and subordination of personal interest to the revolutionary cause.
