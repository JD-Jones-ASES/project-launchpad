Understanding and Defining Religion

Study Slides

Key Concepts and Theoretical Frameworks

Approach. Focus

Characteristics

Functional. What religion *does*

Focuses on the role religion plays in an individual's life, their feelings, and their internal experiences of the divine.

Substantive. What religion *is*

Focuses on the specific content of the religion, such as belief in gods, spirits, or supernatural beings.

Glossary of Important Terms

Religion

An organized system of beliefs, practices, and values used to relate to the sacred or ultimate. It typically includes explanatory stories, moral demands, rituals, and communities of practice.

Theology

The formal and intellectual study of religious beliefs, their meanings, the nature of the divine, and the interpretation of sacred texts.

Transcendence

That which exists beyond the limits of the ordinary, material, or natural world. It refers to the ultimate or sacred reality that many religions point toward.

Functional Definition

A method of defining religion based on the role it plays in human life and the internal experiences it provides.

Substantive Definition

A method of defining religion based on its specific content, such as the belief in supernatural beings or deities.
