Core Concepts and Historical Narrative (1/2)

Teachings

He utilized parables—vivid, short stories involving everyday objects like seeds and coins—to convey his message.

Core Message

He proclaimed the arrival of the "Kingdom of God," described as a new order of justice, mercy, and divine presence.

Followers

He commissioned a circle of twelve close disciples, known as apostles, to spread his message.
