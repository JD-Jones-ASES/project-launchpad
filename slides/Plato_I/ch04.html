Socrates' Central Principle: Never Return Injustice for Injustice

The absolute principle

It is never right to do wrong — not even in response to wrong done to us. If someone harms you unjustly, you are not thereby justified in harming them back.

Why?

Because doing wrong corrupts the soul, and the soul is more important than the body.
