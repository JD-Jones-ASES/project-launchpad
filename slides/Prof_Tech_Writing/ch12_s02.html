Important Terms

Professional Identity

The image and reputation you present to the working world through your resume, online profiles, communications, and professional conduct.

LinkedIn

The world's largest professional social networking platform, used for job searching, recruiting, networking, and sharing industry-related content.

Portfolio

A curated collection of work samples that demonstrates your skills, accomplishments, and professional growth to potential employers or admissions officers.

Digital Footprint

The trail of data you leave online, encompassing all your activities, posts, and profiles. Employers actively screen candidates' digital footprints.
