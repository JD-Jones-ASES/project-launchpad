Important Terms (1/2)

Meeting Minutes

The official written record of what was discussed, decided, and assigned during a meeting. It documents the critical outcomes and serves as a historical record.

Action Item

A specific task or responsibility assigned to an individual or group during a meeting, usually with a clear deadline for completion.

Decision

A resolution, conclusion, or agreement reached by the group during a meeting, often through discussion, consensus, or a formal vote.

Notetaker (Secretary)

The designated person responsible for accurately documenting the meeting minutes, focusing on capturing key outcomes and information.

Facilitator (Chairperson)

The individual responsible for leading the meeting, ensuring the agenda is followed, discussions remain productive, and decisions are reached effectively.

Important Terms (2/2)

Quorum

The minimum number of members legally or constitutionally required to be present at a meeting for its proceedings to be considered valid and for decisions to be officially made.

Adjournment

The formal and official ending of a meeting, typically noted with a specific time.
