Important Terms (1/2)

professional

Relating to a job or career; conducted with competence and appropriate behavior.

technical

Relating to the practical details of a specific field, trade, or subject area.

concise

Expressing ideas in few words, without unnecessary detail.

clarity

The quality of being clear, easy to understand, and free from ambiguity.

jargon

Specialized words or phrases used by a particular profession or group, often confusing to outsiders.

Important Terms (2/2)

deliverable

A tangible or intangible product that must be completed and handed over as part of a project.

draft

A preliminary version of a piece of writing, not yet in its final form.

revision

The process of rereading and improving a piece of writing after the first draft.

Financial Loss

Misunderstood contracts, wasted project time.

Wasted Time

Teams spending days correcting errors caused by unclear communication.

Threat to Lives

Misread medical notes or misunderstood safety procedures.
