Avoid Restating Your Resume

A cover letter should complement, not duplicate, your resume. Instead of listing everything, choose two or three key qualifications and elaborate on them, telling the story behind the bullet points.

Important Terms

Cover Letter

A letter that accompanies a resume or application, introducing the applicant and connecting their qualifications directly to the specific position or opportunity they are seeking.

Tailoring

The practice of customizing a cover letter (or resume) for a specific position by carefully matching your unique qualifications and experiences to the employer's stated needs and the job description.

Key Qualifications

The specific skills, experiences, education, and personal qualities that an employer values most and explicitly states as necessary or desirable for a particular position.

Writing Tip: The "So What?" Test

Weak

"I am a hard worker." (So what? Everyone says that.)

Strong

"I managed a team of five volunteers to organize a community cleanup that collected 800 pounds of litter." (So what? This person gets things done and has leadership skills.)
