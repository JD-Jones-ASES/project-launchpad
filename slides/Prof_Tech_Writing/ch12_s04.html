Quantifiable Results

Uses numbers and percentages (e.g., "2,400+ followers," "increased engagement rate from 3.2% to 7.8%") to demonstrate impact.

Formatting for Impact (1/2)

One Page Rule

For high school students, your resume should always be one page. Condense content rather than shrinking font size excessively.

Consistent Formatting

Maintain uniform styling throughout (e.g., all job titles bolded, all dates right-aligned). Inconsistency looks unprofessional.

Readable Font and Size

Use professional, clean fonts like Calibri, Garamond, Cambria, or Arial. Use 10-12pt for body text and 14-16pt for your name.

White Space

Ensure adequate margins (at least 0.5 inches) and space between sections. Avoid cramming text to the edges.

Bullet Points, Not Paragraphs

Use bullet points for describing experiences and accomplishments. Dense paragraphs are rarely read.

Formatting for Impact (2/2)

No Graphics or Photos

Keep it simple. Avoid visual elements that can confuse ATS software or print poorly in black and white.
