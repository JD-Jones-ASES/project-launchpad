Important Terms (1/2)

Supercilious

Behaving as though one is superior to others; disdainfully proud. (Used to describe Tom Buchanan's manner.)

Levity

Humor or lack of seriousness, especially during a serious occasion. (Nick mentions “hostile levity” as a way of discouraging unwanted confidences.)

Wan

Pale and giving the impression of illness or exhaustion. (Used to describe Jordan Baker's face.)

Complacency

Smug self-satisfaction, especially when unaware of potential dangers. (Applied to Tom's intellectual posturing.)

Peremptory

Insisting on immediate attention or obedience, in a way that allows no refusal. (Describes both Tom's manner and Daisy's call to Nick as he departs.)

Important Terms (2/2)

Fractiousness

Irritability; a tendency toward unruliness. (Nick uses this to characterize Tom's speaking voice.)

Contiguous

Sharing a common border; touching. (Used in the geographic description of East Egg and West Egg.)

Feigned

Simulated or pretended. (Nick says he has “feigned sleep” to avoid others' confessions.)

Rotogravure

A printing process used for magazines and newspaper supplements, especially for photographs. (Nick recognizes Jordan from rotogravure pictures.)

Intimate revelation

A deeply personal disclosure. (Nick describes himself as an unwilling recipient of such confessions.)
