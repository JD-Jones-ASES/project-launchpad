Important Terms (1/2)

Vigil

A period of purposeful wakefulness, often during the night. In this section, Gatsby's all-night watch outside Daisy's house, waiting for a sign from her.

Grail

A legendary sacred object, often sought after in a long and arduous quest. Gatsby's comparison of Daisy to a grail signifies his profound and almost spiritual dedication to pursuing her.

Indirect Characterization

Visual Echo

A recurring image or scene that evokes a previous moment, often to highlight change, continuity, or ironic contrast. The repeated image of Gatsby on the steps serves this purpose.

Careless People

A term Nick uses to describe characters like Tom and Daisy, who cause destruction and retreat into their wealth without consequence. Gatsby is implicitly contrasted with these individuals.
