Important Terms (1/2)

Contiguous

Sharing a boundary or edge; touching.

Anaemic

Lacking vitality or energy; pale and weak.

Hauteur

Disdainful pride; haughty manner.

Proprietary

Relating to ownership; behaving as if one owns something.

Ectoplasm

A supernatural substance supposedly emanating from a spiritualist medium; here used to describe the ghostly quality of McKee's enlarged photograph hovering on the wall.

Important Terms (2/2)

Strident

Loud, harsh, and grating.

Supercilious

Crêpe de Chine
