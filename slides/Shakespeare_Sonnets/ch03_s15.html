Shakespeare's Sonnets: A Reading Companion

Chapter 3, Section 15

Sonnet XV

Study Slides

Key Literary Devices

World-as-stage

"This huge stage presenteth nought but shows" — the familiar Renaissance theatrum mundi metaphor.

Plant metaphor

Men and plants alike governed by the same sky, growing and declining.

Personification

"Wasteful Time debateth with Decay" — Time and Decay as co-conspirators actively plotting against the youth.

Grafting metaphor

"Engraft you new" — poetry as horticultural grafting, inserting the youth's living essence into the rootstock of the verse.
