Shakespeare's Sonnets: A Reading Companion

Chapter 3, Section 7

Sonnet VII

Study Slides

Key Literary Devices

Extended allegory

The sun's daily arc (dawn → noon → sunset) mirrors the human lifespan (youth → prime → old age).

Classical allusion

"Weary car" and "golden pilgrimage" allude to the myth of Phoebus Apollo driving the solar chariot.

Personification

The sun given courtly attributes — "sacred majesty," attended on its "golden pilgrimage."

Pun

"Get a son" — the word "son" resonates against the poem's solar imagery, making sun and son conceptually identical.
