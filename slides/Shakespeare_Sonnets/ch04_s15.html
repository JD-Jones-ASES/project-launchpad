Literary Devices

Personification

"churl Death" as a rude, ungracious figure

Self-deprecation

"poor rude lines," outstripp'd by every pen

Conditional structure

the entire poem is an "if...then" imagining

Embedded quotation

the imagined thought in the sestet and couplet
