Study Slides

Key Literary Devices

Polyptoton / wordplay

The cycling of "love" through multiple meanings in lines 1-6.

Oxymoron

"Gentle thief," "lascivious grace" — beauty and wrongdoing inseparably joined.

Conceit

The moral argument that prior spiritual ownership means the beloved "steals" nothing truly new.

Volta

The couplet pivots from the painstaking argument about grief to a compressed, paradoxical final plea.
