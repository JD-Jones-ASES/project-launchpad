Chapter 6, Section 2

Sonnet XLIV

Study Slides

Key Literary Devices

Conceit

The extended four-element metaphor, drawn from classical philosophy, is the poem's organizing intellectual framework.

Personification

"Injurious distance" is cast as an active harmful agent.

Hyperbole

"thought kills me" exaggerates the mental anguish of realizing he cannot travel like thought.

Volta

The abrupt "But, ah!" at line 11 pivots from fantasy to reality.
