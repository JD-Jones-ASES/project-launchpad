Literary Devices

Extended metaphor

feudal service — lord, vassal, embassage, duty

Metaphor

the poem as a diplomatic letter or embassy

Personification

the guiding star that grants worthiness

Clothing metaphor

"apparel on my tatter'd loving" — love dressed in worthy garments

Volta

at lines 11–12, the turn from current inadequacy to future hope
