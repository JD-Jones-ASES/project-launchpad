Shakespeare's Sonnets: A Reading Companion

Chapter 6, Section 10

Sonnet LII

Study Slides

Key Literary Devices

Simile and extended analogy

Rich man/key, feast days, carcanet jewels, hidden robe — four parallel illustrations of the rarity-value principle.

Metaphor

"captain jewels" — the chief gems as commanders, asserting primacy.

Wordplay

"special instant special-blest" — the repetition intensifies the moment.

Couplet resolution

The paradox of hope and triumph reframes absence as enriching rather than merely painful.
