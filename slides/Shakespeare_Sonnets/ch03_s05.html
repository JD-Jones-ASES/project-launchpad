Shakespeare's Sonnets: A Reading Companion

Chapter 3, Section 5

Sonnet V

Study Slides

Key Literary Devices

Seasonal allegory

Summer = youth and beauty; winter = age and death; distillation = reproduction.

Personification

"Never-resting time" as a relentless agent leading summer to destruction.

Distillation metaphor

The "liquid prisoner pent in walls of glass" — perfume as the preserved essence of summer, analogous to a child carrying the parent's essence.

Verbal neologism

"Unfair" used as a verb — to make unfair or ugly, stripping what "fairly doth excel."
