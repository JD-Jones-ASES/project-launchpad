Study Slides

Literary Devices

Simile (double)

the imperfect actor and the fierce thing replete with rage

Paradox

hearing with eyes; strength weakening itself

Personification

love's "dumb presagers" as advocates

Polyptoton

"more than that tongue that more hath more express'd" — the repeated "more" mimics verbal overload

Volta

at line 9, shifting from problem to proposed solution
