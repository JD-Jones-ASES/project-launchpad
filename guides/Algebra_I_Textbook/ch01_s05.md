# Study Guide: Multiplying and Dividing Real Numbers

This study guide provides a comprehensive overview of the principles and operations associated with multiplying and dividing real numbers, including the handling of signed numbers, zero, and the distribution of negative factors.

---

## Key Concepts and Rules

### 1. Operations with Signed Numbers
The sign of a product or quotient is determined by the relationship between the signs of the two numbers involved.

| Operation | Sign of First Number | Sign of Second Number | Resulting Sign |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Multiplication | Positive (+) | Positive (+) | **Positive (+)** |
| Multiplication | Negative (-) | Negative (-) | **Positive (+)** |
| Multiplication | Positive (+) | Negative (-) | **Negative (-)** |
| Multiplication | Negative (-) | Positive (+) | **Negative (-)** |
| Division | Same Signs | Same Signs | **Positive (+)** |
| Division | Different Signs | Different Signs | **Negative (-)** |

### 2. Properties of Zero
Operations involving zero follow specific constraints:
*   **Multiplication:** For any real number $a$, $a \cdot 0 = 0$ and $0 \cdot a = 0$.
*   **Division of Zero:** For any non-zero real number $a$, the expression $0/a = 0$.
*   **Division by Zero:** Division by zero is never allowed. The expression $a/0$ is **undefined**.
*   **Indeterminate Form:** The specific expression $0/0$ is classified as an indeterminate form, rather than $0$ or $1$.

### 3. The Distributive Property and Negatives
When a negative factor is distributed across a set of grouping symbols, the sign of every term inside the symbols must be changed. For example, distributing $-2$ in the expression $-2(3x - 5)$ results in $-6x + 10$.

### 4. Combining Like Terms
Like terms are identified as terms containing the same variables raised to the same powers. These terms are simplified by adding their coefficients.

## Short-Answer Practice Questions

**Q1. Simplify the expression: $(-3)(-9)$**  
*Answer:* 27 (Same signs result in a positive product).

**Q2. What is the result of $5(-6)(2)$?**  
*Answer:* -60 (Multiply left to right: $5 \times -6 = -30$; then $-30 \times 2 = -60$).

**Q3. Simplify the quotient: $-35 / -5$**  
*Answer:* 7 (Same signs result in a positive quotient).

**Q4. Evaluate the expression: $0 / -8$**  
*Answer:* 0 (Zero divided by any non-zero number is zero).

**Q5. What is the status of the expression $a / 0$?**  
*Answer:* Undefined.

**Q6. Simplify by distributing and combining like terms: $-2(3x - 5) + 4x$**  
*Answer:* $-2x + 10$ (Distribute $-2$ to get $-6x + 10$, then add $-6x$ and $4x$).

**Q7. Simplify: $4x - 3(2x - 1) + 5$**  
*Answer:* $-2x + 8$ (Distribute $-3$ to get $-6x + 3$, then combine like terms: $4x - 6x = -2x$ and $3 + 5 = 8$).

## Glossary of Key Terms

*   **Coefficient:** The numerical factor of a term containing variables (e.g., in $7a$, $7$ is the coefficient).
*   **Combining Like Terms:** The process of simplifying an expression by adding the coefficients of terms that share the same variables and powers.
*   **Distributive Property:** A mathematical rule used to multiply a single term by two or more terms inside a set of parentheses.
*   **Indeterminate Form:** The specific expression $0/0$, which does not have a defined value of $0$ or $1$.
*   **Like Terms:** Terms that possess the exact same variables raised to the exact same exponents.
*   **Product:** The result of a multiplication operation.
*   **Quotient:** The result of a division operation.
*   **Undefined:** A term used to describe an operation that has no mathematical meaning, specifically division by zero. 
*   **Signed Numbers:** Real numbers that are either positive or negative.
