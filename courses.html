About This Catalog

All materials are generated by the Curriculum Factory — a pipeline that converts LaTeX textbooks into multimedia learning experiences using Claude Code, Google NotebookLM, and the YouTube Data API.

Textbooks are CC BY-SA 4.0 licensed. Video content is AI-generated. Quizzes are auto-graded via Google Forms. New courses and chapters are published regularly.

The production system processes textbook content through extraction, script generation, video creation, quiz generation, and automated publishing — turning each chapter into a complete learning module.
