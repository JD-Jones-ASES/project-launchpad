Perspective and Bias

Several of these resources are written from a Classical Christian educational perspective. The author is Catholic, but you will find the books quoting the King James Bible, Martin Luther, and Pope Leo XIII in equal measure.

Claude, as an AI collaborator, tends toward a mainstream consensus view of history — but all perspectives, including mainstream ones, should be examined critically. The science materials discuss evolution. Some of the older literature referenced in these books contains ideas that may feel troubling by 2026 standards.

Approach everything with charity, intellectual curiosity, and a willingness to discuss rather than dismiss.
